Hali ya kuwa kiziwi asiyeona ni nini?

Chama cha Kitaifa cha Viziwi wasiyeona nchini Uganda kinafafanua kuhusu istilahi kiziwi asiyeona kama ifuatavyo:

Hali ya kuwa kiziwi asiyeona ni aina pekee ya udhaifu ambapo mtu ana mchanganyiko wa kiwango cha juu cha udhaifu wa kusikia na kuona.

Definitions of deafblindness from a Kenyan and Tanzanian perspective are not available. Therefore Fasili ya Kinodiki ya kiziwi asiyeona inapanua fasili hii ikizingatia mipaka na vizuizi:

Hali ya kuwa kiziwi asiyeona ni ulemavu wa dhahiri. Hali ya kiziwi asiyeona ni ulemavu wa kuona na kusikia uliokuwa pamoja. Unaweka mipaka katika shughuli za mtu na kumzuia kuwa na ushiriki kamili katika jamii kwa kiwango kwamba jamii hutakiwa kuwezesha huduma maalum, mabadiliko ya kimazingira na/au teknolojia.

Ili kufafanua fasili hii baadhi ya maoni huongezwa.

  1. 1. Uwezo wa kuona na kusikia ni muhimu katika kupata taarifa. Kwahiyo kupungua katika uwezo wa kufanyakazi kwa fahamu hizi mbili ambazo hupeleka taarifa kutoka mbali huongeza uhitaji wa kutumia fahamu zinazokuwa na mipaka kwa kupata taarifa zilizoko jirani (kugusa, maarifa ya mahali na mwendo wa sehemu za mwili yanayokuja kutoka kwenye ogani za fahamu za kwenye misuli na viungo, ufahamu wa kugusa, kunusa na ladha), na pia kutegemea kumbukumbu na kupata hitimisho.
  2. Hitaji la kufanya marekebisho maalum yahusuyo mazingira na huduma hutegemea:
    • muda wa mwanzo katika mahusiano ya ukuzaji wa mawasiliano na kuwa na lugha;
    • kiwango cha ulemavu wa kusikia na kuona, iwapo kimechanganywa na ulemavu mwingine na iwapo kiwango ni imara au ni endelevu.
    and whether it is stable or progressive.
  3. Mtu ambaye ni kiziwi asiyeona anaweza asiwe na uwezo zaidi katika shughuli moja na anaweza kwa kiwango kidogo asiweze shughuli nyingineyo. Kwahiyo kila shughuli na ushiriki wake unapaswa kupimwa kwa njia tofauti. Tofauti katika ufanyaji wa kazi ndani ya kila shughuli na ushiriki wake unaweza pia kusababishwa na hali ya mazingira na visababishi kutoka ndani ya nafsi ya mtu.
  4. Hali ya kuwa kiziwi asiyeona husababisha mahitaji mbalimbali kwaajili ya kufanya mabadiliko katika shughuli zote na hususani;
    • Aina zote za taarifa;
    • maingiliano ya kijamii na mawasiliano;
    • maelekezo ya anga na kuzunguka kwa uhuru;
    • shughuli za maisha ya kila siku na jitihada za kudai;
    • Shughuli zilizo karibu ikiwa ni pamoja na kusoma na kuandika.
    Utengenezaji mwenza ina maana kwamba mtu ambaye ni kiziwi asiyeona na mazingira wanahusika kwa namna iliyo sawa. Wajibu wa jambo hili ili kutokea liko mikononi mwa jamii.
  5. 5. Mbinu ya kitaaluma iliyo miongoni mwa nyinginezo ikiwa ni pamoja na ujuzi unaohusiana na hali ya kuwa kiziwi asiyeona inahitajika katika utoaji wa huduma na mabadiliko ya mazingira.

Identifying and understanding a deafblind person

Very few children are totally deafblind. Usually the child will have residual hearing or very poor vision. Sometimes the child has additional impairments requiring special training. Ideally, the earlier impairment is detected and interventions made, the better chance for the child to overcome the impairment.

If a child has some of these hallmarks, it may be a sign of dual sensory impairment.

  • The child hesitates or stumbles in the stairs or playground.
  • The child avoids or has problems with ball games. He / she may not be able to see the ball and bump on it.
  • The child would like to hold someone in his hand when it gets dark, for example in the evening.
  • The child avoids or withdraws from social situations because it is difficult to keep up with the conversation.
  • The child often complains about headache and becomes quickly tired. He / she needs breaks to get through the day.
  • The child has difficulty following in conversations around the dinner table.

Source: Brochure from National competence service for deafblind people, Norway.

Upimaji

Kwasababu ya utata wa hali ya kuwa kiziwi asiyeona, upimaji ni lazima ufanywe katika ngazi tatu: kitabibu, uwezo wa ufanyaji kazi na katika mazingira ya maisha ya kila siku.
Hata kama uchunguzi wa kitabibu ni muhimu sana, hauelezwi hapa katika tovuti hii.
Upimaji wa utendaji kazi ni pamoja na uchunguzi wa namna fahamu zinavyofanya kazi. Wazazi wanahitaji kuhusishwa katika mchakato huu kwasababu wana uwezo wa kuwasiliana na kutafsiri mtoto anavyoitika katika mazingira tofauti.

Upimaji wa mazingira ya maisha ya kila siku hulenga jinsi mtoto anavyojifunza na jinsi mtoto anavyopata taarifa kutoka kwa watu wengine. Kwa njia ya upimaji huu mtindo wa kujifunza wa mtoto utajulikana.

The development of "Mary"

"Mary" was 7 years old when she was referred for placement. She is a congenital total deafblind child, she cannot walk, chew nor move about in the environment. She objects by resisting screaming, fighting or biting whenever she is introduced to a new activity. Her activities were planned in such a way that they follow one another systematically and always as a routine. After some time, she got used to it and on the contrary to objecting she got used to the activities until sometimes refuse to stop and come out of every activity she participates in.


Chausika . A "case study" from Sense Internatioal

Chausika aged seven, lives in a village in Tanzania She had a tough start to life and as the fifth of seven children there was a lot of competition for attention. Due to her disability she struggled to be a part of the family and was often left alone for long periods of time. When a Sense International support worker first identified Chausika as deafblind she couldn’t walk or eat without support. Her parents had no idea what the cause of her disability was or that she couldn’t see or hear and had given up trying to communicate with her. Over the last two years a support worker has visited the family each week and is teaching Chausika a form of tactile communication to help her communicate with her family. She has made huge progress and is now able to play with her brothers and sisters.

Upimaji wa wanafunzi wapya

Endapo wanafunzi wapya wanaanza katika Kitengo cha Watu ambao ni Viziwi Wasioona cha Kilimani wanapaswa kupitia utafiti ulio kamili ili kutambua njia zao za kuwasiliana, mitindo ya kujifunza na shughuli zipi wanazozifurahia. Kwa kujikita kwenye taarifa hii programu za elimu ya mtu binafsi hutengenezwa.
Msikilize Mary Maragia kuhusu umuhimu wa programu za elimu za watu binafsi.

Mara tu mwanafunzi anapokuja baada ya kuwekwa ndani ya programu yetu, tukiwa kama walimu tunapenda kufanya upimaji unaohusu utendaji kazi. Namna mwanafunzi anavyofanyakazi, jinsi mwanafunzi anavyotumia uono uliosalia, namna mwanafunzi anavyotumia usikivu uliobakia. Namna mwanafunzi anavyofanya shughuli.
Kutoka hapo, mara tu tukishamaliza kufanya upimaji huo basi hapo ndio wakati tunaweza kutengeneza programu ya elimu ya mtu binafsi. Ni muhtasari wa taarifa zinazotokana na kuona kuhusu mwanafunzi. Hivyo, tunachokifanya, hatuwezi kufanyakazi kwa kutegemea wazazi.
Namna mwanafunzi anavyowasiliana, hivyo ni hatua mojawapo katika kufahamu mawasiliano ya mtoto.
Hatua nyingine: ni mtindo upi wa kujifunza, jinsi mwanafunzi anavyojifunza. Hivyo utamwona Louise akiwa ni mtengeneza alama ili aweze kunufaika kutoka kwenye lugha ya alama, kutoka kwenye mwendo, kutoka kwenye fahamu, kutoka kwenye ufahamu wa kugusa.
Hivyo mara tunapokuwa na maarifa hayo jambo linalofuata: Ni baadhi ya shughuli gani ambaye mshirika anaweza kuzifanya na mwanafunzi. Louise anaweza kuogelea, Louise anaweza kufanya mazoezi ya viungo yaliyotengenezwa ili yamfae yeye. Yeye anaujuzi wa kufuma, matibabu kwanjia ya urembo, ujuzi wa kusinga. Hivyo tunapata shughuli ambazo tutamshirikisha mwanafunzi.
Hatua nyingine, kama mshirika, kama mwalimu. Ni jinsi gani nitawasiliana na Louise? Nitatumia ishara ya kugusa, nitatumia mawasiliano yaliyo kamili, nitaitumia anapokuwa haelewi; vitu halisi. Hivyo hiyo ndio aina nyingine ya matumizi.
