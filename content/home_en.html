Sensory Impairment - East Africa

This webportal is about sensory impairement in East Africa. Through text, pictures and video-sequences you will learn about visual impairment, hearing impairment, and dual sensory impairment. Often called deafblindness.

The information goes from simple to complex and may therefore be useful for everyone.

You can choose to read the text in English or Kiswahili. The audio-visual sequences are interpreted in Uganda sign language and given audio description.
