Kutumia kompyuta bila ya kuwa na uwezo wa kuona


The Adaptive Technology Instructor Frida Gatwiri Kiambati at Kenya Institute of Special Education is standing behind some students sitting in front of their computers in a computer lab. She introduces computers to learners who have become blind late in life. They learn to operate the computer without using vision. Some students simulate to be blind wearing blindfolds.
Frida says: Now, we continue. So, we had selected some text. Now we need to copy it to the clipboard. Have you copied already? Use the keys for copying text. Which is?
A learner answers her: Control+C.
A computer screen with text is shown simultaneously the computer reads the text loud.
Frida says: Good. That was the document that we have now.
A closer picture of Frida is shown when she says: So, open or create a new word document. And then we are going to paste whatever we have copied from the previous text to that new location.
A learner with a blindfold and a headset is shown when executing what Frida said.
Another learner with a headset asks: So, there is a copy somewhere?
In the background, we hear Frida’s voice: We have copied from another location. Now we transfer it to a different location.

Frida Gatwiri Kiambati ni Mkufunzi wa Teknolojia Rekebishi katika Idara ya Ulemavu wa Kuona huko KISE. Anafundisha utangulizi wa kompyuta kwa wanafunzi waliokuwa na ulemavu wa kuona katika maisha. Wanajifunza kutumia kompyuta bila ya kuwa na uwezo wa kuona

Sikiliza mahojiano na Frida G. Kiambati

Kitu cha kwanza cha kuwatambulisha na ambacho ni cha mtu binafsi ni kibao cha kompyuta, kwasababu hicho ndicho kinachotufikisha katika kila kitu. Kila kitu ambacho mtu mwenye dosari katika kuona anapaswa kufanya kinapaswa kufanyika kwa kutumia kibao cha kompyuta/tapureta. Huenda ikawa sikutaja kwamba hawatumii monita, pia hawatumii mausi, kwasababu tu kwamba monita inahitaji vielelezo vya kujifunzia ili kwamba mwanafunzi aweze kukagua kinachooneshwa. Kwa wakati huo huo, mausi inahitaji uwezo wa kuona pia, hivyo basi mwanafunzi anaweza kufuata mwendo wa kishale katika skrini. Lakini wanafunzi wangu hawana uwezo wa kuona, hivyo hawana uono huo. Na ndiyo maana tunaiondoa monita na mausi.
Lakini kwa kutumia kibao cha kompyuta au taipureta hatuwatambulishi kuhusu hilo kwa haraka sana, hivyo punde baada ya kufuatilia funguo zote katika kibao, baada ya juma moja au majuma mawili watakuwa wamemudu kujua funguo zote za kwenye kibao
Baada ya kujua funguo zote kwenye kibao, ndipo tunawatambulisha kwenye sehemu nyinginezo za kompyuta na ndipo tutajua zaidi kuhusu utangulizi wa kompyuta. Halafu baada ya hapo ninawatambulisha sehemu ya tatu, ambayo ni utangulizi wa teknolojia rekebishi ili kusudi wanafunzi waweze kuelewa na kuona uzuri wake. Hawawezi kuona na ndiyo maana wanategemea uwezo wao wa kusikia ili waweze kuitumia kompyuta kawaida, kama walivyo watu wanaoona.
Halafu baada ya hapo mafunzo mengine yanafuata kama vile utangulizi wa Word, kwasababu sasa baada ya kujua jinsi ya kutumia kibao vyema, pia wanatambulishwa katika dhana ya teknolojia ya habari na mawasiliano (TEHAMA) na kuanza kutumia mashine ikiwa ni pamoja na kuunga waya. Ninawaambia kushika waya mahali zilipo. Nina waelekeza ili kuelewa namna ya kuunganisha waya ili kompyuta iweze kupata umeme.

Programu maalumu ya kwenye kompyuta iitwayo kisomeo cha skrini na kwenye vinakilishi mpakato.

Kwanjia ya maendeleo ya teknolojia, kompyuta zenye programu fulani zinafanya shughuli tofauti zinazoweza kuwafaa watu wenye dosari katika kuona. Programu hii maalumu inajaribu kutambua na kufasiri kinachooneshwa kwenye skrini. Fasiri hii kisha inawasilishwa kwa mtumiaji ikiwa pamoja na maandiko kwenda kwenye hotuba, ikoni, au chombo cha breli kinachotoa nishati (Chanzo: Wikipedia). Jaws ni programu ya kompyuta inayotumika mahali kwingi lakini ni nyenzo ambayo ni gharama sana. Vinakilishi mpakato kutoka Apple vina mfumo wa utendaji kazi ambao umejengewa humo kwa humo uitwao “VoiceOver.” Hata kama bidhaa za Apple ni gharama kuzinunua. Programu hiyo maalum iliyojengewa ndani kwa ndani zinatolewa bila gharama yoyote na huenda ni nafuu zaidi kuliko kompyuta yenye Windows na ikiwa ni pamoja na programu ya Jaws.

Sauti iliyotiwa ndani ya programu na programu ikiwa ndani ya simu za rununu na tableti.

Miaka ya hivi karibuni simu za rununu na tableti vinafanyakazi ambazo awali zilikuwa zinafanyika kwa kutumia kompyuta za mezani.

  • Ipadi na Iphoni zina programu ya “VoiceOver” pia na programu hiyo katika mazingira mengi inaweza kuchukua nafasi ya Jaws.
  • • Simu za rununu zenye mfumo wa utendaji kazi wa Android zinazo programu maalum iliyotiwa ndani iitwayo "TalkBack". Jaribu kuwa mwangalifu wa hiyo programu kwasababu ubora wa “Talkback” hutofautiana katika apps tofauti.
  1. Nenda kwenye "Settings"
  2. Chagua "Availability"
  3. Chagua " VoiceOver"/"Talkback"
  4. Fungulia "VoiceOver"/"Talkback". (Kwenye Android 4.0 ni lazima urudi na kuamsha ufunguo wa kushika pia).

Tixels kuchukua nafasi ya pixels

Kampuni ya Austria “Blitap” imetengeneza tableti inayochukua nafasi ya skrini ya kugusa ikiwa na kioo cha kugusa. Badala ya pixeli za macho, mapovu madogo (tixeli) yanaibuka kwenye skrini. Skrini ina uwezo wa mistari 14 ikiwa na breli na hata inaweza kuonyesha maudhui ya maandishi. Tableti imegawanyika katika sehemu mbili ambapo maandishi na picha huoneshwa ikiwa ni maandishi yaliyoko kwenye skrini mguso na skrini ya juu kwa ajili ya kushika.

Programu inayogeuza maandishi kuwa kauli vinamfanya mtumiaji kuvinjari kwenye skrini ya kawaida ya kushika. Kurasa za tovuti na pia mafaili ya maandishi na PDF kutoka kwenye kijiti chenye kumbukumbu za taarifa au SD kadi zinazoweza kuoneshwa kwenye skrini ya kushika.

Programu ya “Blitap” ilioneshwa katika Maonesho ya Walaji wa Elektroniki (CES) mnamo January, 2017. Inakisiwa kwamba programu hiyo itapatikana katika maduka baadaye mwaka huu na bei yake inatarajia kuwa ni karibu ya US $ 2,800.



Apps yenye manufaa kwa ajili ya Ipadi, Iphoni na simu za Android

Kwenye Itunes baadhi ya apps ambazo zingeweza kuwa msaada mkubwa kwa watu wenye dosari katika kuona au ulemavu wa kuona, unaweza kupakuliwa.

  • ViA is an abbreviation for "Visually Impaired Apps". ViA has been designed to help identify apps that are useful for adults and children who are blind or have low vision, including those with additional disabilities. When using this app, you can easily do a free search or search different categories, and quickly get a list within your search fields with apps like other visually impaired people around the world have tried and later recommended. The app is a good starting point and it is totally free.

  • Color Reader's Voice tells the colour of an object. Put the iPhone on an object and touch the center of screen, Color Reader's voice tells name of color detected. The app recognize the colours white, light gray, gray, dark gray, black, red, orange, orange yellow, yellow, yellow green, green, cyan, blue, purple, magenta, red, brown and pink. The app is free of charge.

  • VoCal fuses a Dictaphone, Calendar and Alarm system into one. You simply speak your personal memo, set the time and date and you will be reminded in your voice. There is no need to type text! The price for this app will be around 1 USD.

  • Use your phone camera to take a picture of a text. Only a few seconds later the picture of the written text is converted into speech. The "TextGrabber + Translator" app make it happen. The price will be around 1 USD.

  • With Voice Dictation you can use your voice instead of typing. Touch the microphone dictation app, then say what you have to say, the iPhone converts your words into text. Use the dictation to write an email or an SMS messages, taking notes and more. Mostly this app is very good at recognizing words. If you speak clearly the app makes relatively few errors. The price: approximately 1 USD.

  • TapTapSee is designed to help the blind and visually impaired identify objects they encounter in their daily lives. Simply double tap the screen to take a photo of anything, at any angle, and hear the app speak the identification. The app is free of charge.

  • Be My Eyes is an app for Ipad/Iphone. Blind users can request help from a sighted person and the sighted users will then be called for help. As soon as the first sighted user accepts the request for help a live audio-video connection will be set up between the two and the sighted user can tell the blind person what she sees when the blind user points his phone at something using the rear-facing camera. The app is free of charge but the needed video connection is not free. It can be rather expencive tu use this app often.

  • Do you want to learn braille? Then Braille Driller is perfect. It is free and includes a review of the Braille alphabet and four activities of increasing difficulty to help become proficient with it. If you have an Adroid phone you can learn Braille with the "Braille" app. You can browse the Braille table, or take simple quiz for writing and reading Braille. It even support Korean Braille!

  • There are several apps for magnifying a text or picture using the camera in the phone. On Gooogle Play you can choose Magnifying Glassor other similar apps for Android phones. For Ipad and Iphone Magnifying Glass With Light is a proposal. If it is dark you can use the flash as a light. Some of the apps are free, while others cost a small amount.
