Sexuality is one of the sensitive topics especially in African context. Students with visual impairment need to know about their sexuality.

It is important to orient them to biological changes especially during the adolescence period. The effect of not providing knowledge about sexuality to people with visual impairment may lead to more serious problems, for example, teenage pregnancy and low self-esteem.

Also important knowledge about hygiene, self-awareness, sexual rights and abuse need to be discussed with them. Persons with visual impairment need to be encouraged to be confident of themselves and have a positive portrayal of themselves.

The students may need to know the ‘dos’ and ‘don’ts’ in the community.
