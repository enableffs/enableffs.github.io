Recreation and leisure activities are important for people´ health social, and psychological well-being regardless of one’s disability. The activities may vary from:

  • simple to complex
  • cheap to expensive
  • indoor to outdoor
  • individual to group
It is important to evaluate available opportunities before selecting leisure and recreation activities to engage in.

Here are examples of leisure and recreational activities one may choose to engage in:

  • Music listening
  • Dancing
  • Games
  • Picnic
  • Arts
  • Singing
  • Computer games
  • Athletics
  • Story telling
  • Soccer (playing, listening or watching)
  • Swimming
  • Running
