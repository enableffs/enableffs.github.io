Vision and hearing are considered as our most important senses. In this web portal you will find information about sensory impairment.

The information goes from simple to more complex and may therefore be useful for everyone. You can choose to read the text in English or Swahili.

Everyone are welcome to visit the web pages. But everyone who are working for, with or together with people with sensory impairment will benefit from the pages. Much of the content will be important for students of different level.

The web portal is universally designed and the audio-visual sequences are interpreted in sign language and given audio description. There for people with different kind of sensory impairment can benefit from working with the web portal.

Why this web portal?

All the East African nations have ratified the "UN Conventions on the Rights of the Child" which shall promote:

the exchange of appropriate information in the field of preventive health care and of medical, psychological and functional treatment of disabled children, including dissemination of and access to information concerning methods of rehabilitation,education and vocational services...
UN Conventions on the Rights of the Child. Article 23 (1990)

The quote above emphasis the importance of dissemination of information about methods of rehabilitation, education and vocational services. That is the main purpose of this website.
