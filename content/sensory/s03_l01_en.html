The collaborators and financing:

The web portal "Sensory Impairment - East Africa" is a cooperation between:

  • Faculty of Special Needs and Rehabilitation at Kyambogo University, Uganda
  • Kenya Institute of Special Education, Kenya
  • University of Dar es Salaam, Tanzania
  • Faculty of Education, University of Oslo, Norway

The web portal is a part of the NORHED project "ENABLE: Higher Education and Multimedia in Special Needs Education and Rehabilitation " and financed by the Norwegian Agency for Development Cooperation (NORAD).

Development of the technical part

The technical part of the website is mainly developed by Jeremy Toussaint and Richard Nesnass at University of Oslo and Nasser Mugwanya from Kyambogo University who will be responsible for maintenance of the websites.
