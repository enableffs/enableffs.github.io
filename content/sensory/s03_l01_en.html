The web portal "Sensory Impairment" is a cooperation between:

  • Faculty of Special Need Education and Rehabilitation at Kyambogo University, Uganda
  • Kenyas Institute of Special Education, Kenya
  • University of Dar es Salaam, Tanzania
  • Faculty of Education, University of Oslo, Norway

The web portal is a part of the project "Enable" and financed by the Norwegian Agency for Development Cooperation (NORAD).
