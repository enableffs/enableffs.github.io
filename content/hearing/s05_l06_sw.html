There are a variety of devices that can be used by or with person who have a hearing loss.
Examples of the devices are:

Hearing Assistance Technology

These are devices that improve the sound level available for the listener.

  • Hearing aids which are either body worn, placed behind the ear or inserted in the ear
  • Induction loop systems that are used to transmit sound signals
  • Frequency modulation systems - devices that are used to transmit sound on a specific frequency or channel similar to a radio

Communication Support Systems

These are devices used for communication purposes.

  • Alert devices such as door bells that use flashlight and telephones with vibration provisions
  • Teletypewriters - these enable the person with hearing loss to make a telephone call which is automatically transmitted as text through an ordinary phone
  • Video relay services - a type of video telecommunication service which can provide a sign language or spoken language interpretation instantly during communication.
