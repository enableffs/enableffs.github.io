Information about sexual issues for deaf learners

Most of the deaf children don’t get information about sexuality from their parents. Many parents are embarrassed to talk about this issue. And even if they want to talk about it, they might not know how to communicate with their deaf child. This makes it really difficult for the deaf children to learn how to behave when it comes to sexual issues.

Without knowledge about their own sexuality and limits, they are vulnerable to sexual abuse including rape. Deaf children who are raped also have a hard time explaining to their parents and even to the police or other law enforcement officers about what they have been exposed to. And even if they are able to explain it, the police often do not take these issues serious. This is a big problem, but getting a proper education could make the deaf leaners better prepared and able to explain if something happens to them.

Parents should provide sexuality education to their children or engage someone to do it on their behalf.
