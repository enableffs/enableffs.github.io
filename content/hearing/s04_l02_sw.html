Watoto walio na uziwi wanahitaji kuelekezwa kujiandaa kuhusu maisha baada ya shule. Wanahitaji taarifa kuhusu mambo kama hali ya kingono, na usaidizi wa kuendeleza mafunzo ya amali kwa ajili ya kujitegemea.

Ili watoto wenye uziwi waweze kukomaa, kujitegemea, kuheshimika katika jamii, mafunzo ya amali, ukiachilia mbali elimu, ni muhimu.

Mafunzo ya Ufundi

Mafunzo ya ufundi huwawezesha vijana wa umri mdogo wenye uziwi kushindana vilivyo kwenye nafasi za kazi, kujimudu kifedha, na kujitegemea. Kujitegemea huchukua nafasi muhimu katika kukuza hamasa na kuwania fursa sawa kwa ajili ya watu wenye uziwi.

Mafunzo ya ufundi kwa ajili ya watu wenye uziwi yanajumuisha shughuli kama vile: ususi/ufumaji, upishi, ushonaji, useremala, umakenika, na uchomeleaji; ufundi wa vifaa vinavyotumia umeme mdogo na ufundi umeme, upigaji picha za video, muziki, ngoma, na uigizaji.

Ndani ya darasa, kikundi cha wanafunzi wenye usikivu hafifu na viziwi wamakaa sakafuni wakitengeza mazulia. Hawa wanafunzi, wanawake kwa wanaume, wana umri wa miaka kati ya 13 na 14. Baadhi ya wanatumia mashine za cherehani mezani. Mwalimu wa kike anasimamia kazi hiyo, na anawapa ushauri. Mvulana anatengeneza urembo kwa kufuma umbo la lulu. Wavulana wawili wameketi chini wanatengeneza mazulia.

Nje ya jingo mwalimu wa kike anaelezea kinachoendelea kwa kutumia lugha ya alama. :
Tunayo mafunzo ya ufundi katika shule hii kwa sababu baadhi ya wanafunzi, siyo wazuri sana kwa masomo ya darasani, na pia hawanufaiki sana na masomo ya kitaaluma. Kwa hiyo, hali kama hiyo inapojitokeza huwa tunawachukua hawa watoto na kuwapeleka kwenye mafunzo ya ufundi. Na pia huwasaidia katika maisha yao ya baadae. Hiyo ndiyo sababu ya sis kuwa na mafunzo ya ufundi hapa shuleni.

Katika darasa jingine kikundi cha wanatengeneza biskuti. Mwalimu mwingine anasimamia kazi hiyo. Unga unakandwa na kusukumwa.

Mwalimu kiziwi anaendelea kuelezea katika lugha ya alama:
Tunawachagua wachache kwa ajili ya kazi za uokaji, kwa sababu wakati huo huo huwa kuna madarasa ya kitaaluma yanayoendelea. Kwa hiyo, wale ambao hawezi kufanya vizuri kwenye taaluma wanakuja kwenye ufundi, ambao ni uokaji, kutengeneza kei, biskuti na kadhalika

Unga mwingine uliokandwa unasukumwa. Kopo tupu linaweza kutumika kutengenezea biskuti. Kisha biskuti huwekwa kwenye sinia la kuokea, na huwekwa kwenye jiko la kuokea lilijengwa kwa matofali ya kuchoma.

Mwalimu kiziwi anaendelea kufafanua kwa lugha ya alama:
Kwa mfano, kama mtoto amemaliza darasa la saba na hakufanya vizuri kwenye taaluma wanamchukua. Hawana kazi nyingi, lakini lbda pia ni vigumu kwao kufaulu mitihani. Basi wanachagua fani fulani ya ufundi, na kisha wanapata ajira. Pia, tunafundisha ufundi kwa hatua za juu zaidi, kwa hiyo wanawe kutumia mafunzo yao ya ngazi hii kama ya awali, na kisha watajiunga na shule nyingine inayojihusisha na mafunzo ya ufundi pekee.

Shule ya Viziwi ya Uganda hutoa elimu ya msingi pamoja na stadi zinazotangulia ufundi katika useremala, ufundi cherehani, sayansi kimu/maarifa ya nyumbani, na kilimo kwa wanafunzi wasioweza vyema masomo ya kitaaluma.
Nia moja ya mafunzo ya ufundi ya mapema ni kuwapatia stadi kwa ajili ya mafunzo ya ufundi ya ngazi ya juu zaidi ili wanafunzi wapate ajira hapo baadaye.

Utengenezaji wa sandwichi


A group of deaf students in secondary school are preparing sandwiches together with their teacher. They are in a classroom and the other students are sitting and watch the preparation. The teacher communicates with the students using sign language.
Alternating seeing a close shot of teacher and the students preparing the food, the teacher says: When mothers are doing their domestic chores, they talk to their children. But because they don't know how to communicate to their deaf children they kind of leave them. So, this child grows without skills. Basic skills in the home. Here in Africa Children participate. They participate in the home chores. So, you find out that the deaf child is pampered. The mother might feel sorry for the deaf child and won't let them participate. Even then, how will the mother tell them? So, these children lacks skills.
A girl cut a bread into two pieces while the teacher and the other students are preparing salad for the sandwiches.
The teacher says: But simple things like making a vegetable sandwich, which they can even sell as a business when they have grown. I think it is a good skill for them to know. There are many more skills that can be built.
The students who have been watching the preparation now can pick up the prepared sandwiches. Afterwards they sit down and eat the prepared food. They obviously like the food.
The teacher says: So, I introduce such things, then they will go home and ask their mother to by this and that because they saw it be made here. Then they can make something and the parents can appreciate them.
