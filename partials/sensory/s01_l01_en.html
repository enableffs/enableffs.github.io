What is this web portal about?

Vision and hearing are considered as our most important senses. In this web portal you will find information about sensory impairment. Through text, pictures and video-sequences you can learn about visual impairment, hearing impairment and dual sensory impairment, often called deaf-blindness.

Who is this web portal for?

The information goes from simple to more complex and may therefore be useful for everyone. You can choose to read the text in English or Swahili.
The main target group for the web portal is the master students at Faculty of Special Need at Kyambogo University in Uganda.
But everybody else is welcome to visit these web pages. Especially those who are working for, working with or working together with people with sensory impairment. Maybe somebody in your family is impaired? Or somebody you know? Then this web portal can be useful.
People with sensory impairment themselves are of course welcome too, the web portal is universally designed and the audio-visual sequences are interpreted in sign language and given audio description.
