Vocational training for deaf students

The Uganda School for the Deaf offers primary education with pre-vocation skills in carpentry, tailoring, home sciences, and agricultural practice for students who don't fit well in the academic classes, .
One of the intentions of the pre-vocation training is to give the students skills to start their own business in the future.

Production of sandwiches

Many parents don't know how to communicate with their deaf child. They ask their their hearing children do domestic chores, but not the deaf child. The result is that deaf children lack skills i domestic chores.The students in this video learn to make sandwiches. Maybe they one day can make an income by selling sandwiches?
