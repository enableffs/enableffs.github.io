Vocational training for deaf students

The objective is to improve services for Deafblind children and young adults through development of a training program at Kyambogo University in cooperation with Sense International East Africa. In 2011 a baseline study was conducted by an expert of the Royal Dutch Kentalis to start developing the curriculum and a training program for the student trainers of the University. A competence profile was created and based on this profile two senior lecturers of the Kyambogo University followed a three month practical training at Kentalis in the Netherlands. Part of their training was a four week theoretical course ‘Introduction in Communication with people with Congenital Deafblindness’ at the Groningen University under supervision of Kentalis.
