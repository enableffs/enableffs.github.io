In this web portal you will find information about vision and hearing, considered as our most important senses. Through text, pictures and video-sequences you will learn about visual impairment, hearing impairment and combined sensory impairment, often called deaf-blindness. The information goes from simple to more complex and may therefore be useful for everyone. You can choose to read the text in English or Swahili, and the audio-visual sequences are interpreted in sign language and given audio description.
