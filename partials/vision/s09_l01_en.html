There is no effect of visual impairment if the community will be positive to people with visual impairment and be ready to support them. The effects are realized when community hold negative attitudes hence not supportive to people with visual impairment. Therefore effects of visual impairments are mostly due to nurture. However nature is also reported to have effect to congenital visual impairment associated with development and learning which in adulthood life it has been associated with poor quality life. The following can be the effects of visual impairment in the community which hold negative attitude and not supportive:

  • Depression
  • Unemployment hence poor living quality, some organization not prefer to employ people with visual impairment
  • Divorce
  • Sometimes visual impairment combined with other health complications or other disabilities like hearing impairment
