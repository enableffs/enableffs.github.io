Learning experience shows that people frequently been asking questions about visual impairment. Some of these questions intend to find out how to assist people with visual impairment as well as gain knowledge about the whole concept of visual impairment.

Parents may also have questions about how to go about the situation of having a child with visual impairment.

Teachers and students in schools may also have frequently being asking themselves several questions about visual impairment.
