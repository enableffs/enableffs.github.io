Through advancement of technology, computers with certain software are now doing different activities that can be useful to people with visual impairment.

Screen readers

A screen reader is a software application that attempts to identify and interpret what is being displayed on the screen. This interpretation is then re-presented to the user with text-to-speech, sound icons, or a Braille output device (Source: Wikipedia).Jaws is a widely used screen reader


Jaws is a rather expencive tool. Less expencive are Ipads,Ipods, Iphones and laptops from Apple which have "VoiceOver" screen readers as a buildt-in function in their operative system.

Blind students at Kenyatta University in Nairobi use such devices in their learning process. Read more about their experiences.
