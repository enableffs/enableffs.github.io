People with visual impairment have ability to make their career choices. What is important is the support from different stakeholders to make every person with visual impairment meet his/her dreams.

The State shall ensure the progressive implementation of the principle that at least five percent of the members of the public in elective and appointive bodies are persons with disabilities.

The Constitution of Kenya §54.2

The laws in Uganda and Tanzania have other formulations but the meaning is similar to the law above.


Therefore in schools and community in general, people with visual impairment need to have opportunity to career development and training. There should be opportunity to jobs, employment and vocational training.
