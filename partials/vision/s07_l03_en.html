Sexuality is one of sensitive topics especially in African context. Students with visual impairment need to knowledge about their sexuality.

It is important to orient them to biological changes especially during the adolescence period. The effect of not providing people with visual impairment knowledge about sexuality may lead to more serious problems for example teenage pregnancy.

Also important knowledge about hygiene, self-awareness, sexual rights and abuse need to discuss with them.

The students may need to know the ‘dos’ and ‘don’ts’ in the community.
